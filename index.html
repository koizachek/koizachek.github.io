ABOUT

Diana Kozachek researches and develops orchestration principles for Multi-Agent-Systems. Her projects aim to facilitate cognitive flow states and retain critical thinking and problem-solving abilities in humans.

As a writer, she explores alternative configurations of thinking and working in human-AI synergy. Currently, she works on EEG-supported simulation studies.
